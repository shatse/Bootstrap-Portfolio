About Me

Hello! I am an aspiring web developer with a background in education. Born and raised in San Francisco, I moved down to Irvine for college, where I found a love for working with special needs students, and more recently, an interest in programming and web development. Aside from dabbling in teaching and behavioral therapy, I also have a love for dancing, and a background of 16 years of ballet, jazz, contemporary, and hip hop.

As a San Francisco native, I hope to one day work for a company native to the Bay Area. However, my dream companies to work for are Instagram, Google, or Blizzard.
